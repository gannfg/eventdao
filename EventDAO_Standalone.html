FAQ

What is EventDAO?

EventDAO is a platform that verifies real-world events on Solana. Users can submit event claims, stake to support or dispute them, and earn rewards when verified.

How does event verification work?

Each event claim is checked through trusted data sources ‚Äî such as ticket APIs, news feeds, or oracles ‚Äî to confirm whether it truly happened.

What is a Proof of Attendance NFT?

It's a collectible NFT that proves you were part of an event. You can keep it as a digital memory or show it off on-chain.

Do I need crypto to use EventDAO?

Yes, you'll need a Solana wallet and a small amount of SOL for staking or minting. However, we're working on wallet-free onboarding for new users.

Is this gambling or betting?

No. EventDAO uses staking as a way to verify claims ‚Äî not as a form of gambling. It's about truth verification, transparency, and proof of participation.

What kind of events can I submit?

Concerts, festivals, conferences, sports games ‚Äî any real-world event that can be verified publicly.

How do rewards work?

When a claim is confirmed, those who staked correctly share the reward pool. This keeps the ecosystem fair and community-driven.

Why build on Solana?

Solana offers fast, low-cost transactions ‚Äî perfect for event verification and NFT minting at scale.
